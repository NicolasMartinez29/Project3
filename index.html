Space Information

Outer space, also known as the vacuum of the universe, is a vast expanse where celestial bodies, planets, stars, and other astronomical objects are found. Since the beginning of time, humanity has tried to understand this mysterious place. Space missions have provided important discoveries that have helped us understand the origin and evolution of the universe.

In addition to planets and stars, space is also filled with other fascinating phenomena, such as black holes, comets, and distant galaxies. Each space mission expands our knowledge boundaries and brings us closer to understanding how the cosmos works and what our place within it is. Space exploration has allowed us to discover asteroids.
